About Me

My name is Dimas. I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Japanese Language, Literature, and Culture. After graduation, I delved deeper into Japanese language and culture for approximately one year at a language school located in Osaka.

I am a interpreter and language expert with 3 years of experience. Additionally, for the past 2 years, I have been sharpening my skills in graphic design by focusing on creating sign designs for stores. I am experienced in handling projects related to the opening of new stores and the renovation of existing ones.

Furthermore, I have successfully taught myself web design and have created an impressive web portfolio.
